Sausage and Beef Chili

Description

A quick and easy chili, great eating during cold winter months.

Ingredients

  • Olive oil
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 2 green peppers
  • 1 large onion
  • 1 tablespoon of chili powder
  • 1 tablespoon of ground cumin
  • 28 oz canned whole tomatoes, including juice
  • 1 cup salsa
  • 1 cup frozen corn
  • 19 oz canned black beans or red kidney beans
  • 1 half cup of chopped coriander

Instructions

  1. Coat large saucepan with oil and heat to medium-high
  2. Cut sausages in half and squeeze meat out of casings into pan.
  3. Add beef and cook for 7 minutes until no pink remains.
  4. Core, seed, and chop peppers. Coarsely chop onion.
  5. Sprinkle garlic, onion, chili powder and cumin over meat.
  6. Stir until onion softens.
  7. Stir in peppers, tomatoes, salsa, and corn.
  8. Mash the tomatoes inside the pan with a fork.
  9. Bring to a boil, stir often.
  10. Cover, and reduce heat to medium-low.
  11. Simmer, stir occasionally, for about 15 minutes.
  12. Drain and rinse beans, stir the beans into the chili after the chil has simmered for 15 minutes.
  13. Cook until beans are hot, for about 3 minutes.
  14. Stir in coriander.
