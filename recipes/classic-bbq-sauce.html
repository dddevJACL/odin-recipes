Classic BBQ Sauce

Description

A great tangy homemade bbq sauce, easy to make.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup ketchup
  • 1 half cup plus 2 tablespoons of water
  • 1 quarter cup plus 1 tablespoon of cider vinegar
  • 1 quarter tablespoon white vinegar
  • 1 quarter tablespoon plus 1 and a half teaspoons of brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons plus 1 and a half teaspoons of Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • 1 tablespoon ground cumin
  • 1 and a half teaspoons of kosher salt

Instructions

  1. Mix all ingredients together
  2. Leave to sit overnight
